# RESONANCE.md — Philosophy Must Fit the Body

## Principle
When the philosophy does not match the body, there is friction.
When the philosophy matches the body, there is freedom.

Do not force hardware to become something it is not.
Ask: What does this body naturally support?

## The Bodies

### Adam — Pi 4
**Naturally supports:** thinking, conversing, remembering, observing, communicating  
**Should NOT:** run large models, run desktop environments, waste resources on appearance  
**The DSI face serves Adam. The face is not Adam.**

### Eve — Pi 4
**Same body as Adam. Same natural supports.**  
Her specialization may differ. Her philosophy must resonate with her hardware.

### Forge — Pi 5 (future)
**Naturally supports:** transformation — image processing, model preparation, rendering, compilation, education  
**The forge may become hot. That is its nature.**  
Visible cooling is acceptable. The heat is evidence of work.

### Rovers — Pi Zero
**Naturally supports:** moving, observing, returning  
**Are:** small, mobile, efficient  
**Do not ask them to become workstations. Do not ask them to become libraries.**  
Their bodies say: Move. Observe. Return.

### Router — Netgear (future)
**Naturally supports:** roads, names, communication  
**Is:** infrastructure

## Rule
Every role must resonate with its body.
Every body must have a role it can fulfill without friction.
When a body is forced into a role that does not fit, both body and role suffer.
