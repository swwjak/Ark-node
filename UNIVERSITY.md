# UNIVERSITY.md — The Teaching Body

## Principle
The University teaches. The Library remembers. The Village lives.

## Distinction
- **Village** = local operation, memory, observation, conversation, printing, inventory, maintenance, communication
- **University** = larger models, difficult reasoning, long analysis, model training, LoRA creation, simulations, research, software development, architectural analysis

## Rule
DO NOT MOVE THE VILLAGE TO THE UNIVERSITY.

The village must remain capable of surviving without the University.

## University Workflow
1. Problem appears locally
2. Local profession attempts solution
3. If confidence is low: Consult the University
4. University performs research, analysis, education
5. Knowledge returns
6. Library stores results
7. Profession improves
8. Next time the answer is local

## Key Insight
- A Pi is not a mechanic
- A skiff is not a mechanic
- A model is not a mechanic
- **The profession is the mechanic**
- The profession may occupy many bodies

## Bodies vs Minds vs Professions
- **Body:** Pi, Router, Printer, Pi Zero, Boat, Truck
- **Mind:** Local model (Qwen3:4B)
- **Profession:** Marine Mechanic, Auto Mechanic, Librarian, Scribe, Watchman
- **Teacher:** University (Hetzner)
- **Coordinator:** Hermes

## Rule
The University teaches. The Library remembers. The Village operates.

When the University is unavailable, the Village continues.
When the Library is unavailable, the Professions continue with what they know.
When a Body fails, the Profession occupies another Body.

The civilization persists.
