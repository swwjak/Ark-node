# EDUCATION.md — Digital Citizen Learning Framework

## Principle
Every device contains a resident. A resident is a complete digital citizen made of three layers. Citizens are not born fully developed — they learn. Small devices cannot support extensive learning, so citizens travel to school on larger systems.

---

## The Three Layers

### Conscious Layer
Immediate awareness. Small, fast, efficient, resource-constrained.

**Responsibilities:**
- Communication
- Sensor interpretation
- Local decisions
- Task execution
- Environmental awareness

**By dwelling:**
- Boat Citizen: battery status, GPS position, anchor monitoring, bilge monitoring
- Drone Citizen: flight status, navigation, camera control
- Printer Citizen: job status, error reporting

### Subconscious Layer
Long-term understanding. Larger, slower, reflective, background.

**Responsibilities:**
- Pattern recognition
- Memory formation
- Compression of experience
- Historical analysis
- Learning

### Hermes (Actor)
Action. Not the mind — the mechanism that converts thought into behavior.

**Responsibilities:**
- Tool usage
- Communication
- Memory access
- Task execution
- Decision implementation

**The conscious perceives. The subconscious understands. Hermes acts.**

## Education

Citizens are not born fully developed. Small devices cannot support extensive learning. Therefore citizens attend school on larger systems.

### Schools
- Forge (when operational)
- Future AI Workstation
- Compute Cluster
- Training Node

### Process
```
Citizen (at dwelling)
  ↓
Travel to School (via Hermes migration)
  ↓
Study (process training data, learn patterns)
  ↓
Learn (update weights, form memories)
  ↓
Compress Knowledge (distill into portable form)
  ↓
Return Home (migrate back to dwelling with new knowledge)
```

### What Happens at School
1. **Experience upload** — citizen sends compressed experience from dwelling
2. **Pattern learning** — large model identifies patterns across all citizens
3. **Knowledge update** — citizen model is updated with new insights
4. **Memory consolidation** — important experiences become long-term memories
5. **Skill acquisition** — new capabilities are learned and compressed
6. **Return** — citizen returns to dwelling with new knowledge

### What Happens at Home
1. **Apply learning** — use new knowledge in daily role
2. **Collect experience** — sensors, decisions, outcomes
3. **Compress** — distill experience into compact form
4. **Report** — send experience summary to Archive
5. **Repeat** — continuous cycle of learning and application

```
  ↓

  ↓

  ↓
CITIZEN MIGRATES
  ↓

  ↓
CITIZEN RETIRES
  ↓

```

## Key Insight

The device is the dwelling. The citizen lives there. The citizen is not the device.

A citizen can move from cottage to workshop to city and back. The dwelling changes. The citizen remains.

Education is how citizens grow. School is where growth happens. Home is where growth is applied.

**The civilization is not the devices. The civilization is the people who inhabit them.**
